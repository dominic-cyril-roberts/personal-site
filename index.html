Why Hello There!

I'm Nic, and this is a site I made to tell you that! I am a software engineer and burgenoning entrepeneur with 5 years of industry experience working for Fortune 500 companies. I grew up with a genius for a dad, and an incredible listener for a mom, and they taught me everything they know; they did it so well that I think and listen better than they do!

They are also West Indians who were smart enough to finish their education in the USA, where they had me! It also means my brothers and I were disciplined the way they do it in the Third World (once removed). The result of that is that I was finished baking pretty young, but I learned that if you talk out loud sometimes you get threatened or beat! So I've spent most of my adult life with clinical grade depression.

But good news! I recently realized the world isn't patronizing me when they say "my god you're smart". I recently realized the reason so many of my questions frustrate people is because they are not even remotely close to answering them.

Hello World! It's me Nic! I ask hard questions, and I don't stop thinking until I have answers in every language I speak.
